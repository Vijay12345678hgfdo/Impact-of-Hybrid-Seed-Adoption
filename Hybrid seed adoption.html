Socio-Ecological Impact of Hybrid Seed Adoption

Study on changes in land use, farming practices, and biodiversity.

Introduction

This project analyzes how hybrid seed adoption affects farming patterns, land use, and biodiversity in local regions.

Key Findings

  • Change in crop patterns due to hybrid seeds.
  • Increased productivity in some crops, decreased in others.
  • Impact on local biodiversity and soil health.
  • Changes in farmer practices and seed dependency.
